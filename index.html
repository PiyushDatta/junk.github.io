Taniti is a small, tropical island in the Pacific with diverse terrain including sandy and rocky beaches, a small harbor, lush rainforests, and a mountainous interior featuring an active volcano.
Key Facts:
Population, approximately 20,000 (indigenous)
Size, less than 500 square miles

Main Attractions:
Beaches
Tropical rainforest
Active volcano
Taniti City (native architecture)
Yellow Leaf Bay
