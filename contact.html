FAQ:
Where is Taniti? A small island paradise in the Pacific Ocean.
How do I get there? Most visitors arrive by air at Taniti International Airport (+689-555-1212). Some cruise ships also make weekly stops.
What currency should I bring? U.S. Dollars (USD) are preferred, but Euros (EUR) and Yen (JPY) are also accepted by many businesses.
